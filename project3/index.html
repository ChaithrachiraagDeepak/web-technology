About the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most admired architectural masterpieces in the world. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it symbolizes eternal love.

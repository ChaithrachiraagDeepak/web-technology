About Bondi Beach

Bondi Beach, located in Sydney, Australia, is one of the most famous beaches in the world. Known for its golden sands, surfing culture, and vibrant atmosphere, it attracts millions of visitors each year.

Facilities

  • Surfing and Swimming Zones
  • Beachside Cafés and Restaurants
  • Walking and Jogging Paths
  • Lifeguard Services
  • Shops and Souvenir Stalls

Bondi Beach

Bondi Beach is one of Australia's most iconic beaches, famous for its golden sand, surfing waves, and vibrant coastal culture.

Beachfront

- Fun fact: Lifeguards at Bondi are world-renowned and even featured in the TV show "Bondi Rescue".

Bondi Beach

Bondi offers more than just swimming and surfing. The Bondi to Coogee coastal walk is a scenic trail with breathtaking ocean views. Cafes, art galleries, and street markets nearby make it a lively hub for both locals and tourists.
